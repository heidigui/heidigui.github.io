About Me

I am an undergraduate physics student at The Ohio State University interested in astroparticle physics, particle physics, detector instrumentation, and the creative overlap between science and art.

Research

My research focuses on radio detection methods for high-energy neutrinos, including RNO-G airplane background studies, antenna simulations, and signal processing workflows.

Projects

  • RNO-G airplane background identification
  • XFdtd antenna simulations in ice
  • NuRadioMC antenna response studies
  • Scientific plotting and waveform analysis in Python
