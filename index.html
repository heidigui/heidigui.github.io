About Me

I am an undergraduate physics student at The Ohio State University interested in astroparticle physics, particle physics, and detector instrumentation. My research focuses on radio detection methods for high-energy neutrinos.

Research

Projects

  • Antenna response studies with NuRadioMC
  • XFdtd antenna simulations in ice
  • Scientific plotting and data analysis in Python
